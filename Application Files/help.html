How to use Pedal!

1. Sign up for Pedal (if you're seeing this page after downloading Pedal on a mobile device, then skip this step).

2. Pick your memembership package, PedalFree, PedalPass, PedalPass Plus (available on the PedalPass page).

3. Locate your desired Pedal bike using the map, try zooming in and out for more or less detailed views of available bikes.

4. Scan the QR code on your chosen bike.

5. Pay using the Pedal app.

6. Unlock using your bike after paying.

7. Enjoy!
